Speakers

We’re happy to welcome three amazing speakers to present on the most helpful homeschool resources. Prepare for an inspirational extravaganza to advance your homeschooling journey.

Taylor Clark

Schooling looks different for everyone: Curriculum and Lesson Workshops

"The home is the first and most effective place to learn the lessons of life: truth, honor, virtue, self control, the value of education, honest work, and the purpose and privilege of life."

About Taylor

As a homeschool evaluator in Florida and homeschool parent for 6 years, Taylor will be sharing some of her best practices and resources when choosing her curriculum and preparing for a new school year.

  • @taylorclark
  • taylorclark.com

Layla Knight

Less Is More: A guide to unschooling

“Learning can only happen when a child is interested. If he’s not interested, it’s like throwing marshmallows at his head and calling it eating.”

About Layla

As an unschooling home educator for 4 years, Layla is very knowledgeable about the unschooling style to homeschool.

  • @laylaknight
  • laylaknight.com

Jamal Thomas

Homeschool Portfolio: Best Practices

"If the purpose of learning is to score well on a test, we've lost sight of the real reason for learning"

About Jamal

As a home educator and former principal, Jamal will talking about and sharing best practices for annual evaluations and portfolios.
